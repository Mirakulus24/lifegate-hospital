Hospital Overview

Incorporated In 2003

Lifegate Specialist Hospital Ltd has been fully operational for over 20 years. We provide exceptional, multidisciplinary healthcare operations at highly affordable rates. Our unwavering commitment to clinical excellence ensures our parameters match globally trusted standards.

Because of our rigorous dedication, our diverse client base extends across the entire country and reaches cleanly into the international diaspora community.

Licensing Note: Fully accredited and licensed to operate comprehensive facilities across both Lagos State and Anambra State.
